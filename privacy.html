Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

OGENTI, Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates the ogenti desktop application and ogenti.com website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data — Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Usage Data — Anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency. No personal data or agent content is transmitted.
  • Agent Data — All agent data, configurations, and conversation logs are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the ogenti service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To improve the product experience
  • To communicate important updates

Data Storage

ogenti is a desktop-first application. Your agent data, chat history, and configurations remain on your local machine. Our servers only handle account authentication and credit transactions.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) for agent intelligence. When agents interact with these services, only the minimum required context is sent. We do not store or log prompts on our servers.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit. Since agent data is stored locally, you have full control over its security.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at contact@ogenti.com.
