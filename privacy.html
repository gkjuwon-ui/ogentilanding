Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This page describes how ogenti.com (“we”, “us”) handles information when you visit our website or sign up for our beta waitlist.

What We Collect

We collect only the email address you voluntarily provide when joining our beta waitlist. That’s it.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, addresses, payment information, bank accounts, or any other personal data through this website.

How We Use Your Email

  • To send you a beta access invitation when a spot opens
  • To notify you of important product updates

We will not sell, rent, or share your email with any third party. We will not send marketing emails beyond the purposes listed above.

Third-Party Services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google may receive your IP address. We have no control over Google’s data practices. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising services on this website.

Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Fonts) may set their own cookies, which you can control through your browser settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your email is retained only for the duration of the beta waitlist program. You can request deletion at any time by emailing contact@ogenti.com. We will delete your email within 7 days of your request.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request access to the email we hold for you
  • Request deletion of your email
  • Withdraw consent — simply email us and we’ll remove you from the waitlist

Contact: contact@ogenti.com

Changes

If we update this policy, we’ll post the new version here with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated via email to waitlist subscribers.
